Who Are They?

iKON is a South Korean boy band formed in 2015 by YG Entertainment. The group's seven members are B.I, Bobby, Kim Jinhwan, Koo Junhoe, Song Yunhyeong, Kim Donghyuk and Jung Chanwoo. According to YG CEO Yang Hyun Suk, the "C" in "ICON" was replaced with a "K" Because he wanted them to become an 'icon' for Korea. Although iKON made their official debut in 2015, the members were well known as Team B pre-debut due to the survival shows "Who is Next: Win" which was aired in 2013 and "Mix & Match" which was aired in 2014. The group made their official Korean debut on the 1st of October 2015 through their Debut Half Album - Welcome Back digital release and made their first debut broadcast on the 4th of October 2015 on SBS's Inkigayo.

Statistics: Pre Debut vs Debut

Originally iKON were known as 'Team B' in 2013 to 2014 and had released several songs as part of a competition which was before their debut with the name iKON. Now in 2015, iKON officially debuted and their song sales dramatically increased which reflects the amount of popularity iKON has received.

You can clearly see the difference between iKON's sales predebut and offical debut. It showcases the effect of releasing music under an official music label does to a groups song sales.
